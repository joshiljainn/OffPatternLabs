## Where to Find Newsletter Founders

### 1. **Substack**
- Browse by category: Business, Tech, Finance
- Look for: Last post date 3+ months ago
- URL pattern: `[newsletter].substack.com`

### 2. **Beehiiv**
- Browse their publication directory
- Look for inactive but high-subscriber lists

### 3. **Twitter/X**
- Search: "taking a break from newsletter"
- Search: "newsletter on hiatus"
- Search: "burnt out from Substack"

### 4. **IndieHackers**
- Forum posts about newsletter struggles
- MRR posts showing newsletter revenue

### 5. **LinkedIn**
- Search: "newsletter founder"
- Search: "Substack writer"
- Filter by: Posted in last 30 days

## Daily Outreach Goals

| Activity | Target | Time |
|----------|--------|------|
| **DMs sent** | 10-15/day | 30 min |
| **Follow-ups** | 5-10/day | 15 min |
| **Valuations sent** | 3-5/week | 1 hour |
| **Calls booked** | 2-3/week | 1 hour |

**Math:**
- 15 DMs/day × 5 days = 75/week
- 75 DMs → 15 responses (20%)
- 15 responses → 5 valuations (33%)
- 5 valuations → 2 calls (40%)
- 2 calls → **1 deal** (50%)

**1 deal @ $50k acquisition or 30% rev share = $15k+**

## Objection Handlers

**"I'm not ready to sell yet"**
```
Totally get that. Selling isn't the only path — we also do revenue share partnerships where you keep full ownership. We monetize, you get paid, no sale needed.

Either way, happy to send over a free valuation so you know what you're sitting on. Knowledge is power.
```

**"What's the catch?"**
```
Fair question. No catch — we make money when you make money. If we can't grow your revenue, we don't get paid. That's why we're selective about which newsletters we partner with.

The free valuation is genuinely free. No strings.
```

**"I want to keep creative control"**
```
100% — you keep it. We handle monetization (sponsors, affiliates, products). You keep writing (or not writing) however you want. We're the engine, you're the driver.
```

**"How do I know you're legit?"**
```
Great question. Check out offpatternlabs.com — you can see our full process, philosophy, and what we've done.

Also happy to hop on a quick call with zero pressure. You can vet us as much as we vet the newsletter.
```
