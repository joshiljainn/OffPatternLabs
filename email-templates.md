## Quick Reference: When to Use Each

| Template | Use When |
|----------|----------|
| **Email 1 (Dormant)** | First contact, newsletter inactive 3+ months |
| **Email 2 (Not Monetized)** | Active newsletter, no/low monetization |
| **Email 3 (High-Value)** | Business/Tech/Finance niche, 10k+ subs |
| **Follow-Up 1** | 3-4 days after initial, no response |
| **Follow-Up 2** | 7 days after initial, no response |
| **Follow-Up 3** | 14 days, "breakup email" |
| **Request for Info** | They expressed interest |
| **Valuation Delivery** | After you've analyzed their list |
| **Call Invitation** | After they review valuation |
| **After Good Call** | Sending formal proposal |
| **After They Sign** | Onboarding new partner |
| **Nurture 1-3** | They said "not now" — staying in touch |

---

**Pro Tip:** Personalize every email. Mention their newsletter name, a specific article, their niche. Generic = spam. Personal = conversation.
