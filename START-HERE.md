## 🛡️ Protection Against Backdoor Deals

1. **NDA before revealing seller details** — Standard in M&A
2. **Non-circumvent agreement** — 12-month standard
3. **You control the intro** — Never reveal full details until commitment
4. **Reputation** — OffPattern Labs brand is on the line

## 📞 Your First Call Script

```
**Opening (2 min)**
"Thanks for hopping on. Walk me through [Newsletter Name] — when did you start, what's worked, why the pause?"

**Discovery (5 min)**
- What's your current subscriber count?
- When was the last post?
- Have you ever monetized? If yes, how much?
- What's your goal here? (Cash out? Passive income? Just tired?)

**Pitch (3 min)**
"Based on what you've shared, here's what I'm thinking...
[Explain revenue share or acquisition model]
We handle [X], you keep [Y], you get [Z]."

**Next Steps (2 min)**
"I'll send over a formal proposal by [date]. You review, we chat again, and if it feels right, we move forward. Sound fair?"
```

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Waiting for perfection** — Send imperfect DMs now, optimize later
❌ **Over-explaining** — Keep DMs short. Curiosity closes deals.
❌ **Skipping follow-ups** — 80% of deals happen after 3rd touch
❌ **Undervaluing** — 5k subs in business niche = $25k-100k+ asset
❌ **Doing it alone** — Use the AI scout, use the templates, systemize
